Our Journey in Industrial Wood Packaging

Established in 1984, Wood Products Unlimited has evolved into an industry leader in custom industrial wood packaging solutions. Our journey began with a focused mission to provide superior wood crating solutions and has grown into a comprehensive industrial packaging enterprise serving global clients.

Today, we specialize in ISPM 15 certified export crates, custom industrial crating, and international shipping packaging solutions. Our continuous innovation and unwavering commitment to quality have established us as a trusted partner for businesses worldwide.
