About Wood Products Unlimited

Four Decades of Excellence in Industrial Wood Packaging

Our Story

Founded in 1984, Wood Products Unlimited has been at the forefront of industrial packaging solutions for over four decades. What started as a small wood crating operation has grown into a leading manufacturer of custom industrial packaging solutions, serving clients across the globe.

Our journey has been marked by continuous innovation, unwavering commitment to quality, and dedication to meeting the evolving needs of our clients in various industries.

Mission & Values

Our Mission

To provide innovative, reliable, and sustainable wood packaging solutions that protect our clients' valuable products throughout their journey.

Our Vision

To be the global leader in sustainable industrial packaging solutions, setting industry standards for quality and innovation.

Core Values

Quality, Innovation, Sustainability, Customer Focus, and Excellence in everything we do.

Custom Engineering

Our team of experts designs and engineers packaging solutions tailored to your specific requirements, ensuring maximum protection for your products.
